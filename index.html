Frequently Asked Questions

How are points calculated?

You receive 1 point for a new user registration and 5 points when a referred user upgrades to a Yearly Pro subscription.

What are "Pending" vs. "Available" points?

To ensure system integrity, all points enter a 72-hour pending period. After 72 hours, they become "Available" for redemption. This protects all users and ensures rewards are verified.

How do I redeem my points?

Once you have 12 Available points, the $25 Digital Gift Card option will become active in your "Referrals & Rewards" dashboard. The delivery is fully automated and instant.

Is there a limit to how much I can earn?

There is no limit. The more high-value professionals you bring to Hello!, the more you earn.
