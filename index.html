Frequently Asked Questions

How are referral points earned?

You receive 1 point for every referred user who signs up for a Page Pack and a substantial 5 points when a referred user upgrades to a Yearly Pro subscription.

How are rewards redeemed?

You can redeem points for valuable in-app perks like a Dark Theme (1 Point) or an extra Signature Page (3 Points). The primary goal for monetization is the $25 Digital Gift Card, which is automatically available for redemption once you reach 12 Available Points.

What is the difference between "Pending" and "Available" points?

To ensure system integrity, all points enter a 72-hour pending period for verification. After this period, they become "Available" for redemption.

Is there a limit on referrals?

There is no limit. We encourage you to share Hello! with any professionals in your network who could benefit from a more dynamic and effective networking tool.
